The legendary Smokey Nagata

Smokey Nagata, whose real name is Susumu Nagata, is a Japanese automotive enthusiast known for his involvement in the underground racing scene.

He gained notoriety for his high-speed antics on public roads, particularly in the UK, where he was involved in several high-profile incidents. One of his most famous stunts was driving a heavily modified Toyota Supra at extremely high speeds on the public streets of the UK

Smokey Nagata owns a Twin-Turbo V12 swapped Supra that pushes over 2000 HP. A few pictures of the supra and the engine are shown below.

Since 1994, Nagata and the Top Secret team have participated in speed trials and speed challenges such as 0-249 mph, 0-186 mph, 1000m, and top speed challenges, often with a Toyota Supra.

Smokey Nagata lives on as a legend and will forever be remembered as a highly skilled driver and street racer.
